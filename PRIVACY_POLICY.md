# Privacy Policy for FidelTele

Effective Date: February 5, 2026

FidelTele ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

- **SMS Messages:** The App requests access to your SMS messages solely to read and parse balance, plan, and notification messages from your mobile network provider. We do not access, collect, or store any personal or unrelated SMS messages.
- **No Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, contacts, or call logs.

## How We Use Information

- The SMS messages accessed are used only locally on your device to display your balances, plans, and notifications within the App.
- No SMS data is sent to our servers or any third party.

## Data Sharing

- We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties.

## Permissions

- The App requests SMS permissions strictly to provide its core functionality of reading and displaying your balance and plan information.
- The App does not request unnecessary permissions.

## Security

- All data is processed locally on your device. We do not store or transmit your SMS data externally.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App.
